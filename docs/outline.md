## 8. Contact Us Page Structure

I want to keep the same basic structure and fields on the Contact US page, but I’d like you to modernize the design and optimize it for a high conversion rate. Here’s what I’m looking for:

I want the same fields I already have (name, email/phone, message, etc.).  
Keep the same general purpose: simple way to request a quote or ask a question.  
Use a simple, clean, mobile-first layout with plenty of white space.  
Align the form in a single column (especially on mobile) to make it easy to complete.  
Make the Submit / Request a Free Quote button large, bold, and easy to tap on mobile.  
Make it very mobile-friendly, big input fields, large tap targets, and no horizontal scrolling.  
Add a short headline, “Tell us about your project, Request a Free Quote for Your Parking Lot or Facility”  
Add bullet points next to or under the form, such as 1- year Warranty on All Work, Commercial & Industrial Specialists, and Minimal Disruption to Your Operations  
Show a small “⭐ 5.0/5 Google Rating” near the form.

### Confirmation and tracking

After submitting, show a clear success message on the page: “Thank you! We’ve received your request and will contact you shortly.”

If possible, set up tracking so we can measure:  
Form submissions as conversions and Click-to-call taps (on mobile)
